About Me

They say the sky is vast yet what we admire are the sparkling stars Much the same way , I wish to become on of those stars that twinkles in the vast sky of programming. Since play school days I often saw my uncle before the computer and considered it nothing more than a toy but with time the realization came that he was actually coding so I used to stand by his side to understand what this device was.Who knew my fascination would turn into passion .I wrote my first computer program using logo at the age of eight,as time passed from logo the programming journey continued to basic, C, C++,Core Java,SQL,HTML,Python... . During my higher secondary school days, I participated in a seminar related to cyber security and bagged the first prize for two consecutive years in a row ,making way into the school magazine’s achievement section , this event made me realize programming has to be my career track.

The reason I wish to become a software developer is in one of my seminars someone said, the disadvantage of computer technology is ,it doesn’t help society morally, I intend to change this, technology has bridged numerous gaps then why not in the field of culture and morality.
